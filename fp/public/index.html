About Us

Grocery Delivery

Foodport enables neighbors to deliver groceries to each other! Those who need groceries, the Buyers, can easily order groceres that will be delivered by those already going to the store, the Porters.

Human Connection

Food connects people. It's just that simple.

Environmental Savings

By essentially carpooling groceries, users can save transportation costs and the environment; it's truly a win-win situation!
